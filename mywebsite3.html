Owning a very large garden is not as easy as it seems, which is why most hire gardeners to look after their large sized gardens. A large garden requires a lot of work, such as mowing grass, pruning trees and fertilising flowers/plants. This is quite difficult to do, especially when having a very bussy schedule. Even though it has it complications, it is worth having a lot of space, because this makes it possible to get furniture, which fit without decreasing the gardens size or causing it to look it is very important to plan ahead before starting to work/design a garden, because this will prevent you spending unnecessary costs. An example of this is buying too many tiles, designing rocks or fruit trees. Less is more!
